Services

Individual Counselling

Personalized one-on-one sessions to address personal, emotional, or psychological challenges in a supportive and confidential environment.

Couple Counselling

Guided sessions for partners to improve communication, resolve conflicts, and strengthen relationship.

Family Counselling

Supportive sessions involving family members to address and resolve conflicts, improve communication, and strengthen relationships within the family unit.

Grief & Bereavement

Support and guidance for individuals coping with loss, helping navigate the grieving process and finding ways to heal.

Trauma Counselling

Counselling to help individuals recover from past traumatic experiences and regain a sense of control and well-being.

Stress Management

Techniques and strategies to help individuals cope with and reduce stress, promoting overall well-being.

Employee Wellness

Comprehensive initiatives designed to promote the health, happiness, and productivity of employees through various activities, resources, and support services.

Intervention Programmes & Psycho-Education

Structured sessions addressing issues and behaviors, providing tools for positive change and better mental health.

Psychometric Assessments

Tests to evaluate aptitudes, interests, and personality for informed school subject and career choices.
